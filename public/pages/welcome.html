What Applinic is
To the physician, Applinic means many things. Generally it is an online Health Information and Management System that connects him or his practice to the global pool of patients, providing both parties with a platform for communication using contemporary technological tools such as online appointment scheduler, instant messaging chats, audio and video calls as well as video conference.

It also provides space for other health service providers such as the pharmacists, the laboratory scientists and radiologist or imaging scientist to weigh in their professional care on the patient as requested by the doctor, such that even the physician without a center can practice his profession by accessing these services on the platform for the welfare of his patients.

The platform creates a virtual case note for each patient so that the doctor can click to view all his patients, review their case notes, fix or accept an appointment request and provide professional opinions use any of several means of communication available on the platform. He may also use Applinic to send investigation requests to laboratories or radiology centers, receive results and reports and write or send prescriptions to pharmacies for an ongoing patient management.

He may also refer patients to appropriate consultants on the platform for a more specialized care, visit his e-wallet account to see his earnings and cash out as much as he wants whenever he so desires without any restrictions or limitations.
Benefits and Features
Applinic confers lots of benefits to the practitioner. Below is an inexhaustive outline of these:

i.Builds the doctor’s patients base and practice.
ii.Makes the practice and skills more visible and accessible to seeking patients and colleagues.
iii.Reduces patronage of quacks and unauthorized, untrained health care providers.
iv.Reduces foreign medical tourism and helps the locals and the economy.
v.Generates income through consultations, treatments, procedures and loyalty rewards.
vi.Provides direct chat ( text, audio, video ) with patients, pharmacists, laboratories and radio-diagnostic practitioners.
vii.Provides free web-based dashboard with profile, notifications, appointment requests, Patients Waiting Room (PWR), publications (procedure write-ups and videos), doctors chat rooms, e-wallet, etc.
viii.Enables review of doctor’s consultation fee from his dash board at will.
ix.Same day cash out into your bank account.
Practice Requirements
Doctors who intend to use Applinic for medical practice are required to meet with a few requirements.

i. A current verifiable practicing license and registration with the relevant national/international professional bodies for both practice and specialization.

ii. Acceptance to grant a discount of 10% of the consultation fees to Applinic as well as 12.5% of all fees/bills for admissions, investigations, treatments, surgeries and other procedures to the platform. Please see Terms and Conditions for details of this requirement.

iii. All payments for services must be made and received through the MediPay e-wallet of the platform.

iv. Notes of management and treatment plans must be kept in patients’ case files and a medical report written at the end of each case management for the patient’s electronic medical records.

Sources of Patients

ii.Direct requests from patients.

iv.Referral from other doctors and health providers.

The Patients’ Waiting Room (PWR)
The PWR is the most viable source of patients. All complaints from patients who don’t have personal doctors or who do but are looking for a new type of specialist are anonymously published in the waiting room. Doctors visit the PWR to review the published cases and respond to those that pertain to their specialties or areas of interest. Each doctor’s response to a case may be accompanied by his consultation fee but this is discouraged. Consultation fees are better presented after the doctor has presented his opinion and management plan to the patient. This helps to build a relationship of trust and establishment of confidence before the bill is issued.

Appointment Scheduler
Of absolute necessity, many patients need to be physically seen by the doctor, examined and treated. Doctors can use the appointment scheduler to fix or grant appointments to patients for both onsite and online consultations.

This utility also keeps the doctor abreast of the appointments he has each day or week from the comfort of his handheld device for better planning and preparation.
Online Multimedia Communication
Applinic is equipped with free instant messaging and audio and video calls which doctors can use for chats with patients to consult online, with colleagues to review cases, and with pharmacists or diagnostic centers to discuss patients, make inquiries or obtain clarifications. Video Conference

Manage On-The-Go
Applinic enables doctors access the electronic folders of all their patients on the go from any internet enabled device including mobile phones and tablets. When patients call for counsels or make complaints, the doctor will be able to open their Applinic accounts and quickly review his previous consultations with them before he can more properly advise them.

He doesn’t have to go to the clinic first to consult the patient’s case note before he can be updated. The results of the laboratory and radiological investigations he requested in a previous appointment can be accessed there as soon as they are ready.

This way the doctor is enabled to serve his patients quicker and better by being enabled to react more promptly to abnormal results of investigations irrespective of his location. For example by being enabled to immediately receive and review a recent laboratory result and prescribe treatment for the new finding or change antibiotics because of a revealing sensitivity result without waiting for the next appointment with the patient which may be in a few days, the doctor is empowered to more quickly save lives and to better serve the patient through the use of Applinic than through conventional practice.
Earn Money
By positioning doctors to be more easily accessible by a global pool of patients, Applinic increases their opportunities to profit both conventionally through onsite consultations, treatments and procedures as well as virtually via online sessions, follow ups and telemedical managements.

Besides these opportunities, doctors can also bank financial revenue on the platform by earning loyalty rewards when they request investigations or write prescriptions for patients via the platform and the patients pay for the services in diagnostic centers and pharmacies through their Applinic e-wallets. Loyalty rewards are equivalent to monies which can be cashed out at will. This incentive enables doctors to earn passive income while engaging in routine practice.

At the same time, it encourages their participation in the Electronic Patients Record (EPR) campaign which seeks to promote all patients on the globe having easily accessible medical records for better care. That is the ONLY way the application confirms the services were received.

Doctors may also receive income from Applinic through other means such as participation in special treatment services organized by Self, Applinic or third parties, delivery of health seminars and webinars, publication of articles which attract populous readership, participation in clinical trials outsourced through the platform and through other modalities currently under development.
