What Applinic is to the Doctor
To the physician, Applinic means many things. Generally it is an online Health Information and Management System that connects him or his practice to the global pool of patients, providing both parties with a platform for communication using contemporary technological tools such as online appointment scheduler, instant messaging chats, audio and video calls as well as video conference. It also provides space for other health service providers such as the pharmacists, the laboratory scientists and radiologist or imaging scientist to weigh in their professional care on the patient as requested by the doctor, such that even the physician without a center can practice his profession by accessing these services on the platform for the welfare of his patients. The platform creates a virtual case note for each patient such that each of the service providers can render his service from his location and make requisite inputs while the physician in the center can review results of investigations, write prescriptions and treat or refer patients to appropriate consultants on the platform for a more specialized care. Each registered physician has a secure account he can log into to see his patients, review their case notes and communicate with those he is interested in using the means he prefers for communication among the lot on the platform. The doctor may also, visit his e-wallet account to see his earnings and cash out as much as he wants whenever he so desires without any restrictions or limitations.
Become more Visible and Accessible
Patients are constantly in search of doctors and specialist with certain skills and abilities even in their own cities and countries but many times don’t find them. This leads many to seek solutions beyond their own boundaries of city, state or country. Applinic provides patients with a platform to find the doctor he desires based on specialty, location and skills. Doctors registered and practicing on Applinic will be more visible and accessible because they will be listed on the patients’ searches depending on the specialty and location filtered by the patient. This way doctors can be of service to more patients who need them than they currently are both in their location and beyond. This will also reduce the patients’ sometimes frustrating manual search, travels and expenditures.
Grow Your Practice
By being of service to many more patients than he ordinarily would, APPLINIC helps the doctor grow his practice more rapidly than the conventional way as his patients’ base expands the APPLINIC way. All patients that have ever been treated by the doctor are profiled on his dashboard such that he can search, view or access them at any time at will.
Enjoy Free Account, Dashboard Management Software
Upon creation of an Applinic account, you will receive a free dashboard and access to our online software engine for managing your practice. This application will help you manage most of your practice such as patient registration, appointment scheduling, laboratory and radiology investigation requests, diagnostic result accessibility and drug prescriptions. Other functionalities available on your dashboard include virtual case notes for documentations of your patient clerking in standard format, medical reports, buttons for acceptance of requests from patients for consultations, chats, audio and video calls and for referrals of your patients to other colleagues. You will also enjoy the privilege of updating your profile from here, including your special skills and procedures so that when patients or doctors search for those procedures or the diseases they treat, your name/practice will be listed. You may also upload pictures and videos in line with the procedures.
Online Multimedia Communication
Applinic is equipped with free instant messaging and audio and video calls which doctors can use for chats with patients to consult online, with colleagues to review cases, and with pharmacists or diagnostic centers to discuss patients, make inquiries or obtain clarifications. When put to proper use, this real time communication can transfer the information that will save lives.
Host or Participate in Video Conference
Applinic also provides free video conference service that can connect doctors with their patients and other stakeholders such as patient’s relatives, other doctors, pharmacists and diagnostic practitioners to discuss the patient together as a team to better understand the case and agree on how to best manage him. The video conference is also open for other applications.
Access, Review and Manage On-The-Go
Applinic enables doctors access the electronic folders of all their patients on the go from any internet enabled device including mobile phones and tablets. When patients call for counsels or make complaints, the doctor will be able to open their Applinic accounts and quickly review his previous consultations with them to be updated on the case so he can more properly advise them. The results of the laboratory and radiological investigations they requested will be sent there as soon as they are ready. This way the doctor will be enabled to serve them quicker and better by reacting more promptly to abnormal results irrespective of their location. For example by immediately seeing a recent laboratory result and prescribing treatment for the new finding or changing antibiotics because of a revealing sensitivity test result without waiting for the next appointment with the patient, the doctor will more surely save and better serve the patient through the use of Applinic than through conventional practice.
Earn Money by Using the Platform
By positioning doctors to be more easily accessible by a global pool of patients, Applinic increases their opportunities to profit both conventionally through onsite consultations, treatments and procedures as well as virtually via online sessions, follow ups and telemedical managements. Besides these opportunities, doctors can also bank financial revenue on the platform by earning credit scores when they request investigations or write prescriptions for patients via the platform and the patients pay for the services in pharmacies and diagnostic centers through their Applinic e-wallets. Credit scores are equivalent to monies which can be cashed out at will. This incentive enables doctors to earn passive income while engaging in routine practice. At the same time, it encourages their participation in the Electronic Patients Record (EPR) campaign which seeks to promote all patients having easily accessible medical records for better care. Patients’ medical records which include medical reports, results of investigations and doctors’ prescriptions are kept in Applinic ONLY IF the payments for them are made through the e-wallet for that is the way the application confirms the services were received. Doctors may also receive income from Applinic through other means such as participation in special treatment services organized by Applinic or third parties, publication of articles which attract populous readership, participation in clinical trials outsourced through the platform and through other modalities currently under development.
Patient Pooling
Specialists with certain skills can also use Applinic to pool patients with certain conditions together in any city of choice to consult, treat or provide specific procedures for them. This service can be requested by clicking here. Terms and Conditions apply.
Use the Appointment Scheduler
Of absolute necessity, many patients need to be physically seen by the doctor, examined and treated. Doctors can use the free appointment scheduler to fix or grant appointments to patients for both onsite and online consultations. This utility also keeps the doctor abreast of the appointments he has each day or week from the comfort of his handheld device wherever he is for better planning and preparation.

The Patients’ Waiting Room (PWR)
The PWR is the most viable source of patients. All complaints from patients who don’t have personal doctors or who do but are looking for a new type of specialist are anonymously published in the waiting room. Doctors visit the PWR to review the published cases and respond to those that pertain to their specialties or areas of interest. Each doctor’s response to a case may be accompanied by his consultation fee but this is discouraged. Consultation fees are better presented after the doctor has chatted and counseled with the patient and explained his plan/strategy/approach to the case to build a relationship of trust and establish confidence before issuing a bill.
Platform Commissions
If the patient and the doctor both agree on a consultation offer, the patient’s account will be debited and the doctor’s credited before the treatment commences. The system then gives the doctor and patient a link to communicate by chat, audio or video call as they desire, or an appointment for a physical consultation. The Applinic commission is deducted at source.
Help Customers Open Accounts
Some customers who don’t have an Applinic account may come for your services. The system allows you to create a quick account for them in just two steps so that an electronic record can be kept of their medical reports for your future references and theirs. This also enables their enlisting in your online patients’ list.
Your Rating
When patients search for doctors with specific skills and procedures or for specialists, the search result is usually listed according to the rating order of the professionals who fall within the searched category. Professionals with the highest ratings come tops in the list and are more likely to be seen and patronized. Several activities increase the rating of health service providers on the platform. Every health service provider on Applinic can be rated by a customer after a session or transaction. Positive feed-back ratings by patients after treatment or consultation sessions with them are the highest contributor to the overall ranking. Opening accounts for patients also similarly attracts a high rating score.
Rent Consulting Room

Request For Diagnostic Investigations
Applinic partners with the best laboratories and radio-diagnostic centers in the world. Use the appropriate online forms to request investigations for your patients and send the request to any center of your choice closest to the patient. The patient will need to visit the laboratory for the sample collection. Laboratories who offer home or offices services may also be contacted on patients’ request. Always encourage patients to pay with their e-wallet. Loyalty rewards accrue for physicians when patients pay for investigations from platform wallets. Such rewards are credited to the doctor’s Applinic account for subsequent cash out.
Write Paperless Prescriptions
Following online or onsite consultations, doctors can write prescriptions for patients using the paperless prescription sheets on the platform. Prescriptions for patients can be serviced by partnering pharmacies who operate within the patient’s city. Doctors may use the search function to find drugs on the Applinic platform by first identifying the pharmacies that have them in stock before forwarding the prescription. That way the patients don’t have to suffer looking for the drugs. Alternatively the patient may forward the prescription himself if the doctor so approves.
When patients pay for the drugs with their e-wallet, doctors earn loyalty rewards as explained above (See the section titled “Earn Money By Using the Platform”) and the prescription registers in their medical records. If they pay cash, the system cannot confirm the drugs were procured so the prescription will not be recorded in the patient's’ record. More so, loyalty rewards will not be earned.

You need to also remind the diagnostic centers to write/enter the results of your investigations through APPLINIC.

Agree on this with the doctors, pharmacies, medical and diagnostic centers before you patronize them, even if you are receiving the treatment abroad.
Access To Your Medical Records
Applinic automatically creates a free Electronic Patients Records for you. This contains your doctors’ reports, prescriptions and results of diagnostic investigations you paid for through your Applinic e-wallet. You can access this record at will from your dashboard for free.

If you release this token to him, he will be enabled to review your past medical history just once and be better informed about your health so he can offer you the best treatment. Even though there is a penalty for the violation of patients’ privacy rights, Applinic will not be held responsible for what the doctor does with your medical records. We are currently working

on the graded access to limit the type and details of your medical information the doctor will be able to view with each granted access.

In addition records of all medical reports, prescriptions drugs and results of investigations paid for through Applinic are automatically created in the user’s electronic medical records and secured for future access.

You can only use this functionality once after each treatment session or transaction.
Confidentiality and Security
The confidentiality and security of your health information and e-wallet are of premium importance and priority to us. Consequently we have taken measures to ensure that no one else can access these information aside you and those you authorize. The Secure Socket Layer (SSL), encryption of all health and financial information as well as anti-malwares installed in our servers are a few of these. You also have some responsibilities towards your account security.

In the first place, you are advised to create your account with your Nick Names instead of your given names. Secondly do not put up your real pictures if you don’t want your health care providers to know who they are treating. You may conduct a video chat with them if you desire to disclose your identity or show them something in your body just for that moment. Please ensure you do not share your log in details with others not even your health care providers. You may issue a One Time Password (OTP) generated from your phone to grant your doctor a one-time access to your medical records during a treatment session but please do not share this OTP with any other. You are advised to use all other means you know to help secure your vital and confidential health information while we do our part. Applinic will never ask you for your log in details or private health information and will not be held responsible for any disclosure of your information to a third party as a result of your less than utmost security care with your account details.

Update Your Stock Regularly
It is highly advisable to update your drug inventory for three reasons. Firstly, Applinic has in-built drug-search functionality so doctors, patients and other customers will often search for drugs online before going to buy them. If your inventory is not updated and a search for a drug you have is done, the system will not list your store in the search result of those who have it, so your chances of sales will be reduced. Secondly, if you no longer have the stock of a drug and your inventory shows you do because you didn’t update, the customers who visit your store to buy the drug based on the search result will be disappointed when they discover you don’t have it and the next time they see your store in a search result, they may be skeptical to visit again. Thirdly, when your stock of specific drugs/products is depleted below your set threshold, the potential suppliers will see that and bid for the supply. This way you get competitive bids from interested suppliers which will enable you to replenish almost immediately and at lower costs. Consequently you never run out of stock and your profit increases because of cheaper cost prices.

Help Customers Credit Their E-Wallets

This is done by direct transfer from your account to theirs in exchange for their cash. Helping customers credit their e-wallet accounts in exchange for their cash will earn you high rating points. In addition you get quick cash when you need it need without cashing out.

When patients search for centers that perform specific diagnostic services, the search result is usually listed according to the rating order of the centers which provide the searched service within the selected area. The higher the rating of your practice, the more it will come tops in this search. Your center is more likely to be patronized if it’s on top. Our field staff verification report of your center rates you based on the standards of your center and practice as observed during their visit. Details of the parameters used for this rating is provided below. Also every health service provider on Applinic can be rated by the patient or customer after a session or transaction based on his or her experience. Opening accounts for patients and funding their e-wallets also similarly attract high rating scores.

Your e-wallet account keeps a record of your inflows, transfers and expenditures. Check your transaction history to see these details. You can filter your search by date period of the transaction.
Cash Out
To cash out just click on the Cash Out button and fill the form to indicate the amount of money you want to cash out and the bank account you want the money transferred to if you had not filled this before. For security reasons, this will require a one-time password sent to your phone to confirm you initiated this request.
Help Customers Credit Their E-Wallets
Customers who don’t have money in their e-wallet account to pay for your services but have cash can be assisted. The system also allows you to fund their e-wallet for them. This is done by direct transfer from your account to theirs in exchange for their cash. Helping customers credit their e-wallet accounts in exchange for their cash will earn you high rating points. In addition you get quick cash when you need it need without cashing out.
Rent your Consulting Rooms for a Fee and Patronage
Laboratories who have consulting rooms may rent it to doctors for consultation. This service has at least three rewards - a rental fee may apply which is a percentage of the doctor’s consultation/professional fee; patronage – laboratory investigations requested for the patient will be done at your center as long as you offer the services and thirdly the opportunity for a development of a long lasting mutually beneficial professional relationship with the doctor exists for your center.
Your Rating
When patients search for centers that perform specific diagnostic services, the search result is usually listed according to the rating order of the centers which provide the searched service within the selected area. The higher the rating of your practice, the more it will come tops in this search. Your center is more likely to be patronized if it’s on top. Several factors influence the rating of health service providers on the Applinic. Our field staff verification report of your center rates you based on the standards of your center and practice as observed during their visit. Details of the parameters used for this rating is provided below. Also every health service provider on Applinic can be rated by the patient or customer after a session or transaction based on his or her experience. Opening accounts for patients and funding their e-wallets also similarly attract high rating scores.

What is Applinic to the Radiologist and Imaging Scientist?
To the radiologist and imaging scientist, Applinic is an online Health Information and Management System that connects him to patients and customers from all over the world, providing him with a platform for communication with them using contemporary technological tools such as instant messaging, audio and video calls as well as video conference. It also enables him to profile his services and tests in our database which enables patients see his center in the list of those who provide such services when the patient searches for that test or service. This helps the patient avoid wastage of time and resources in a blind grope for who provides what tests and where. Upon sign up, each diagnostic center gets an e-wallet account for reception of payments, management of transactions and cashing out of monies.
Be Listed Among the Real Professionals
Registration of diagnostic radiology practices in Applinic follows a verification that will filter out non-professionals. So only valid and licensed diagnostic facilities owned or backed by qualified and board certified radiologists and imaging scientists who provide standard diagnostic services are registered. This way, the platform sifts the wheat from the tares. Your enlistment and operation on Applinic means you are among the cohort of radiologists and imaging scientist the new generation of enlightened patients and customers will patronize and deal with in this new digital age. This is a privilege you must cherish and take full advantage of by using the many features and opportunities available on Applinic to grow your business and optimize services. We wish you a profitable business relationship with Applinic.
Grow Your Business
Applinic will pool more customers to your practice than you currently serve. By receiving and attending to more patients through this online portal, you can grow your business beyond your current rate as new customers patronize you.
Free Dashboard and Pharmacy Management Software
Upon creation of an Applinic account, you will receive a free dashboard and access to our online software engine for managing your practice. This application will help you manage most of your center activities such as patient registration, appointment scheduling, radiology request selection, result publication and forwarding to doctor/patient as well as financial accounting, procurement and online record keeping of all your laboratory results for future references. You can also view your e-wallet account, transfer money, cash out and view/print transactions.
Online Multimedia Communication
Available on Applinic are free instant messaging and audio and video calls which radiologists and imaging scientists can use for chats with patients for explanations, colleagues for collaborations and doctors for clarifications and inquiries. When put to proper use, this real time communication can transfer the information that will save lives.
Be Online Always
You need to be logged in always with your computer or mobile device so you can see and respond to chat inquiries and other business/service requests that may present. Patients, doctors, pharmacists and other customers are more likely to patronize you when you are online than when you are not.
