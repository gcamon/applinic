Find Drugs This will search for drug(s) name you entered in all registered and verified pharmaceutical stores on applinic in ascending order of availability. By default your city is used but you can search in other cities or simply keep the city field empty to search in all cities in the country. Also you can search for multiple drugs at once by clicking the '+' to add more fields.You can search with prescription ID when such option is available.
