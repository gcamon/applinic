GENERAL TERMS AND CONDITIONS 
Platform Charges

There are two options for payment by physicians and hospitals who use Applinic services depending on preference. They are Annual Subscription charges and Discount Percentages. While annual subscriptions are paid once every twelve (12) months, discount percentages are ongoing charges paid for using the platform on a pay-as-you-go basis.

Subscriptions

Healthcare Providers who desire to subscribe annually for the service pay the fixed amount for one year to use all the services. Applinic management reserve the right to review this amount either direction without prior notice to the customer. The reviews will not affect existing subscriptions but will take effect at the expiration of the existing subscription when the user intends to renew it.  For now the cost of subscription is $150 (N54,750) per annum for a corporate hospital account and $70 (N25,550) per annum for a personal physician's account. Tariff plans for less duration are available for both types of providers.

Discount Percentages

This is charge per consultation fee paid by the patient.  The current rate is Ten Percent (10%) and is deductible each time the patient pays through the Medi-pay wallet. Cash payment for consultation by patients is prohibited and healthcare providers should discourage this.  If for any reason the patient is unable to pay via the wallet, the hospital or physician may collect cash, while Applinic relies on their integrity and honesty to remit the money to the platform using the internal Medipay transfer service.  Circumventing, diverting, evading, by-passing and sidestepping of patients’ payments through other channels aside the platform wallet without making remittances is a violation of the terms of usage of this application and may attract sanctions. The current percentage can be reviewed at anytime without prior notice but a one month moratorium will be granted to all providers before this takes effect.
