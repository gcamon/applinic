What Applinic is to the Doctor
To the physician, Applinic means many things. Generally it is an online Health Information and Management System that connects him or his practice to the global pool of patients, providing both parties with a platform for communication using contemporary technological tools such as online appointment scheduler, instant messaging chats, audio and video calls as well as video conference. It also provides space for other health service providers such as the pharmacists, the laboratory scientists and radiologist or imaging scientist to weigh in their professional care on the patient as requested by the doctor, such that even the physician without a center can practice his profession by accessing these services on the platform for the welfare of his patients.


The platform creates a virtual case note for each patient such that each of the service providers can render his service from his location and make requisite inputs while the physician in the center can review results of investigations, write prescriptions and treat or refer patients to appropriate consultants on the platform for a more specialized care. Each registered physician has a secure account he can log into to see his patients, review their case notes and communicate with those he is interested in using the means he prefers for communication among the lot on the platform. The doctor may also, visit his e-wallet account to see his earnings and cash out as much as he wants whenever he so desires without any restrictions or limitations.
